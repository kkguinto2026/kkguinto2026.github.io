About Us

Established in 1987 by Daniel Sorranto and Ellen Harper, we have been determined to assist small businesses in growing. We provide sufficient sponsor to enable success. As of March 2021, we have helped over 300 businesses, and some have even went international! We want to help the community economics, and we will continue that for years to come.
